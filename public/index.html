Herron-Morton Place Oktoberfest

September 28th, 2013. 2-10pm

Enjoy beer, hard cider, mead, food trucks, live music and more at the third annual Herron-Morton Place Oktoberfest. The event will take place in the historic Herron-Morton Place park and all proceeds go towards the park endowment.

Food & Beer

Trust your tastebuds on this one. We've invited some of our favorite food trucks, restaurants and a few neighborhood joints to bring their tastiest food and treats to Oktoberfest this year.

So whatever fits your fancy, whether it's pizza grilled to perfection in front of your face, authentic german brats, or phenomenal pho, we've got something for you at Oktoberfest.

  • Scratch Truck
  • Byrne's Grilled Pizza
  • Pho Mi
  • Joy Of All Who Sorrow Brats
  • One Eleven Cakery
  • Flat 12
  • Fountain Square
  • New Day Meadery
  • Sunking
  • Indiana City

Music



A hand-picked selection of bands, from Indianapolis and beyond, will be rocking out at the park all day - so make sure to bring your dancing shoes. Last year's set included favorites like Pravada, a local band featuring former members of Margot & The Nuclear So Sos, and James and The Drifters, Fort Wayne's answer to The Fray.

Stay tuned as we update the band list.
