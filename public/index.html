Herron-Morton Place Oktoberfest

September 28th, 2013. 2-10pm

Enjoy beer, hard cider, mead, food trucks, live music and more at the third annual Herron-Morton Place Oktoberfest. The event will take place in the historic Herron-Morton Place park and all proceeds go towards a park endowment.
